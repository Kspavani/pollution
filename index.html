water pollution

Water pollution (or aquatic pollution) is the contamination of water bodies, usually as a result of human activities. Water bodies include for example lakes, rivers, oceans, aquifers and groundwater. ... Water pollution is the leading worldwide cause of death and disease, e.g. due to water-borne diseases.
